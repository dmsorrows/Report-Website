AMASDA Previously Recorded Sites (Templates for each Chapter throughout Report)

 

Option 1. There are Sites Within 1 Mile

 

For ABSTRACT

 

A review of the Automated Management of Archeological Site Data in Arkansas (AMASDA) database produced 8 previously recorded archeological sites within a 1.6 kilometer (km) (1 mile [mi]) radius of the Project Area. The site nearest the Project Area is 3PU0838, located approximately 0.23 km (0.14 mi) from the perimeter of the Project Area.

 

For INTRODUCTION

 

A review of the AMASDA database produced 8 previously recorded archeological sites within a 1.6 km (1 mi) radius of the Project Area. The site nearest the Project Area is 3PU0838, located approximately 0.23 km (0.14 mi) from the perimeter of the Project Area.

 

For BACKGROUND RESEACH

 

Flat Earth Archeology conducted background research and studies of the Project Area prior to fieldwork. Devin Sorrows, RPA searched the records in the archeological site database, AMASDA Online, maintained by the Arkansas Archeological Survey (ARAS) in Fayetteville to check for previously recorded archeological sites within one mile from the Project Area (AMASDA 2022). There were six previously recorded archeological sites within this search area (Table 1). The site nearest the Project Area is 3PU0736, located approximately 1.08 km (0.67 mi) from the northern perimeter of the Project Area.
