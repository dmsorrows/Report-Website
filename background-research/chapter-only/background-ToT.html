Trail of Tears (Background Research Chapter Only)

 

Option 1. Project Area Is Within 1 Mile Trail of Tears Route

 

Trail of Tears National Historic Trail

 

A review of the Footprints Across Arkansas: Trail of Tears Removal Corridors for the Cherokees, Chickasaws, Choctaws, Creeks & Seminoles by Amber M. Horne indicated that the Project Area lies near a corridor of the Trail of Tears National Historic Trail (Figure 22). The nearest removal route is situated roughly 0.38 km (0.24 mi) northeast of the Project Area. Additionally, parties of Cherokees, Chickasaws, Choctaws, Creeks, and Seminoles were removed along these proximal routes (Horne 2006).
